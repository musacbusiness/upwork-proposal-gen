## 1. Algorithm Fundamentals

### How LinkedIn Ranks Posts in 2026

LinkedIn ranks posts by **relevance, expertise, clarity, and early engagement**â€”not recency alone.

**The 3-Stage Distribution Process:**
1. **Quality Filtering** - LinkedIn checks if post meets quality standards
2. **Small-Audience Testing** - Shows to ~10% of your network first
3. **Content Relevance Scoring** - If engagement is high, expands reach

### The Golden Hour
**First 60-90 minutes** determine whether LinkedIn expands your post's reach. Early engagement (especially comments) signals "this post is worth showing to more people."

### What the Algorithm Rewards
| âœ… Rewards | âŒ Penalizes |
|------------|--------------|
| Native content (no external links) | External links in post body |
| Meaningful comments | Engagement pod activity |
| High dwell time (people reading) | Overly promotional content |
| Expert-led frameworks & insights | Generic/low-value content |
| Carousels, documents, native video | Posts with <3 or >5 hashtags |

### Key Statistics
- Posts with images: **2.3x more engagement** than text-only
- Carousels: **24.42% engagement rate** vs 6.67% for text
- Comments are **worth more than likes** in algorithm scoring
- Native videos: **20x more shares** than other content types

### Character Limits & Recommendations

| Element | Limit | Optimal |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Total post | 3,000 chars | 1,000-1,500 chars |
| Hook (before "See more") | 210 chars | 150-200 chars |
| Hashtags | 30 max | 3-5 |
| Image alt text | 120 chars | Full 120 chars |

### Formatting Best Practices

**DO:**
- Use generous line breaks (white space)
- Break up walls of text
- Use bullet points sparingly (3-5 max)
- Keep paragraphs to 1-2 sentences
- Use emojis strategically (1-3 per post)

**DON'T:**
- Write long paragraphs
- Use more than 5 hashtags
- Include external links in post body
- Overuse emojis (looks unprofessional)
- Write in ALL CAPS

### Post Length by Purpose

| Purpose | Length | Why |
|---------|--------|-----|

| Story/lesson | 1,000-1,300 chars | Dwell time + engagement |
| Framework/guide | 1,300-2,000 chars | Authority building |
| Carousel | 8-12 slides | Deep engagement |

## 4. Hook Templates

### The First 2 Lines Determine Everything

**90% of your post's performance depends on your opening line.**

The hook must appear **before the "See more" cutoff** (210 characters).

### Hook Categories & Templates

#### 1. **Curiosity Hooks**
```
"The #1 mistake I see business owners make with AI..."
"What nobody tells you about automation..."
"I've reviewed 200+ businesses. Here's the pattern I keep seeing..."
"This one change increased my productivity by 300%..."
```

#### 2. **Controversial/Contrarian Hooks**
```
"Unpopular opinion: [conventional wisdom] is wrong."
"Stop doing [common practice]. It's killing your growth."
"Everyone says [X]. I disagree."
"The advice you've been given about [topic] is outdated."
```

#### 3. **Story Hooks**
```
"Last week, a client asked me something that stopped me cold..."
"I almost made a $50,000 mistake..."
"3 years ago, I was working 80-hour weeks..."
"The worst business decision I ever made taught me..."
```

#### 4. **Number/Data Hooks**
```
"I analyzed 500 LinkedIn posts. Here's what works..."
"This automation saves businesses $47,000/year..."
"93% of [group] don't know this about [topic]..."
"I spent $10,000 testing this. Here's what I learned..."
```

#### 5. **Question Hooks**
```
"What would you do with an extra 10 hours/week?"
"Why do most automation projects fail?"
"Are you making this common AI mistake?"
"What's stopping you from scaling right now?"
```

#### 6. **Bold Claim Hooks**
```
"You don't need more employees. You need better systems."
"AI won't replace you. Someone using AI will."
"The businesses that thrive in 2026 will have one thing in common..."
"I can predict which businesses will fail. Here's how..."
```

#### 7. **Relatable Hooks**
```
"Raise your hand if you've ever [common frustration]..."
"If you've ever felt [emotion] about [topic], read this..."
"You're not alone if [common struggle]..."
"Most entrepreneurs won't admit this, but..."
```

### Hook Formulas

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Number + Benefit | "3 automations that save me 10 hours/week" |
| Mistake + Consequence | "This AI mistake is costing you clients" |
| Story + Stakes | "I almost lost my biggest client. Here's why..." |
| Contrarian + Proof | "Hustle culture is a lie. Data proves it." |
| Question + Intrigue | "Why do 80% of automations fail?" |

## 5. Visual Guidelines

### Image Dimensions

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Best For |
|--------|------------|--------------|----------|
| Feed post (landscape) | 1200 x 627 px | 1.91:1 | Links, general |
| Feed post (portrait) | 1200 x 1350 px | 4:5 | Mobile-first, max real estate |
| Feed post (square) | 1200 x 1200 px | 1:1 | Consistent branding |
| Carousel slide | 1080 x 1080 px | 1:1 | Multi-slide content |
| Carousel (vertical) | 1080 x 1350 px | 4:5 | Mobile-optimized carousel |

### Image Design Best Practices

**Colors & Branding:**
- Use consistent brand colors across all posts
- High contrast for readability (especially on mobile)
- Avoid overly busy backgrounds
- Professional, clean aesthetic

**Text on Images:**
- Keep text minimal (LinkedIn compresses images)
- Use large, readable fonts (min 24pt)
- Leave 60-80px padding from edges
- Contrast text against background

**What Works:**
- Bold statistics/numbers
- Simple diagrams/frameworks
- Before/after comparisons
- Headshots with text overlay
- Clean infographics

**What to Avoid:**
- Stock photos (look generic)
- Too much text (illegible on mobile)
- Low contrast (hard to read)
- Cluttered designs

### Carousel Best Practices

**Structure:**
1. **Slide 1**: Hook slide - compelling question/statement
2. **Slides 2-10**: Value delivery - one point per slide
3. **Final Slide**: CTA - clear next step

**Design Rules:**
- Consistent fonts, colors, margins throughout
- One key point per slide
- Large, readable text
- Progress indicators (e.g., "3/10")
- CTA on final slide

**Engagement Stats:**
- Carousels get **24.42% engagement** (vs 6.67% text)
- Each swipe = engagement signal
- 8-12 slides optimal
- Educational content performs best

## 7. Content Mix Strategy

### The 40/40/20 Framework

**40% Expertise Posts** (Authority Building)
- Frameworks and systems
- How-to guides
- Industry insights
- Data and research
- Lessons learned

**40% Engagement Posts** (Reach Building)
- Questions and polls
- Hot takes and opinions
- Relatable content
- Behind-the-scenes
- Personal stories

**20% Promotional Posts** (Lead Generation)
- Case studies with results
- Testimonials and social proof
- Direct offers
- Service explanations
- Booking CTAs

### Weekly Posting Schedule

| Day | Post Type | Purpose |
|-----|-----------|---------|
| Monday | Framework/How-to | Authority |
| Tuesday | Story/Lesson | Engagement |
| Wednesday | Data/Insight | Authority |
| Thursday | Question/Poll | Engagement |
| Friday | Case Study + CTA | Leads |

### Content Pillars for AI/Automation Authority

1. **AI Implementation** - Practical prompts, tools, techniques
2. **Automation Strategies** - Systems, workflows, efficiency
3. **Business Transformation** - Before/after, results, case studies
4. **Industry Insights** - Trends, predictions, analysis

## Quick Reference Checklist

### Before Posting:

- [ ] Hook is compelling (first 210 chars)
- [ ] Body delivers clear value
- [ ] Formatting is clean (line breaks, bullets)
- [ ] CTA matches post purpose
- [ ] 3-5 relevant hashtags
- [ ] No external links in body
- [ ] Image/carousel optimized for mobile
- [ ] Posted during peak hours (8-10am or 12-1pm)

### Engagement Tactics:

- [ ] Respond to every comment within 1 hour
- [ ] Ask follow-up questions in replies
- [ ] Like and reply to comments on other posts
- [ ] Post consistently (3-5x per week minimum)
- [ ] Mix content types (text, carousel, video)

### Lead Generation Flow:

1. **80% of posts**: Build authority + engagement (soft CTAs)
2. **20% of posts**: Direct lead generation (booking CTAs)
3. **First comment**: Place links and direct CTAs here
4. **DMs**: Follow up with engaged commenters
5. **Booking**: Use Calendly or similar for frictionless scheduling
