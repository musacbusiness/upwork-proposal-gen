## HOOK TYPES: Pick One

**Need immediate result?** Use **Quantified Data**
> "72 meetings booked using this method"

**Want lots of comments?** Use **Contrarian**
> "Everyone says daily posts are essential. I grew 50k followers with 2 posts per week."

**Building trust?** Use **Vulnerability**
> "I got rejected 12 times before I understood what actually works."

**Driving curiosity?** Use **Open Loop**
> "I discovered something by accident. It changed everything."

**Quick engagement?** Use **Question**
> "Yes or no: Is daily posting necessary?"

**Positioning authority?** Use **Framework**
> "Here's the 3-step system I use..."

## POST LENGTH GUIDE

| Length | When to Use | Performance |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| 150-300 chars | Quick takes, reactions | Good for engagement |
| 400-800 chars | Most posts (SWEET SPOT) | Consistent high performance |
| 1,200-1,800 chars | Deep insights, stories | Peak engagement potential |
| 2,000+ chars | AVOID | 35% drop in engagement |

**Rule:** Write what the idea deserves. Don't pad or compress for word count.

## THE FIRST 60 MINUTES (What to Do)

**Minute 0-5:** Post goes live

**Minute 5-15:**
- Get likes/comments from your network
- Start replying to comments

**Minute 15-30:**
- Active author engagement
- Reply to every comment
- Ask follow-up questions
- Show genuine interest

**Minute 30-60:**
- Continue engagement
- Monitor momentum
- Adjust if needed

**Minute 60+:**
- Lighter touch
- Check back periodically

**Key insight:** Your engagement in first 60 minutes determines algorithmic reach.

## WRITING TIPS (In 60 Seconds)

1. **Hook first** - Make first 2 sentences stop scrolling
2. **Story before solution** - Emotional resonance before advice
3. **Be specific** - 72 people, not "many"; January 2024, not "recently"
4. **One idea** - Don't try to teach 5 things
5. **Short sentences** - Especially in hook. (Like. This.)
6. **Question everything** - "Is this specific?" "Does this feel authentic?" "Would I click 'see more'?"
7. **Read aloud** - Does it sound like you?
