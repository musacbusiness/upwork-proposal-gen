### Tier 2: Strong Engagement (Use 30% of the time)

#### 4. **Personal Photos (You in the image)**
- **Engagement Rate**: Up to 30% higher with relevant selfies
- **Why it works**: Human connection, authenticity
- **Best for**: Behind-the-scenes, celebration posts, personal stories

**Types that work:**
- You at your desk/workspace
- You presenting/speaking
- You with clients (with permission)
- Candid work moments
- Conference/event photos

**Avoid:**
- Low-quality selfies
- Overly posed/fake photos
- Stock-looking images

#### 5. **Quote Cards (With Original Insights)**
- **Why it works**: Easy to consume, shareable
- **Best for**: Thought leadership, memorable takeaways
- **Note**: Generic motivational quotes = scroll past. **Original insights only.**

## Video Content Strategy

### Video Engagement Stats
- **36% year-over-year growth** in video viewership
- **Native videos shared 20x more** than other content
- **LinkedIn Live: 7x more reactions, 24x more comments**
- **Video usage up 69%** in 2025

### Optimal Video Lengths

| Length | Best For | Performance |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **5-15 seconds** | Quick tips, hooks | Highest completion |
| **30 seconds** | Product overviews, talking head | Sweet spot for ads |
| **30-60 seconds** | Most organic content | Optimal engagement |
| **60-90 seconds** | Tutorials, explanations | Good for value content |
| **2-3 minutes** | Deep dives, detailed content | Works if highly valuable |

**Key insight**: The first **3 seconds** determine if people watch. Hook immediately.

### Video Types That Perform Best

#### 1. **Talking Head + B-Roll Combo** â­
- **Structure**: You speaking + clips of what you're discussing
- **Why it works**: Personal connection + visual interest
- **Example**: "Here's how I automate my week" + screen recordings of tools

#### 2. **Screen Recordings with Narration**
- **Structure**: Show your screen while explaining
- **Why it works**: Demonstrates expertise, teaches practical skills
- **Example**: Walking through an automation workflow

#### 3. **Quick Tips (Under 60 seconds)**
- **Structure**: One actionable tip delivered fast
- **Why it works**: Consumable, shareable, high completion
- **Example**: "The one prompt that saves me 5 hours/week"

#### 4. **Behind-the-Scenes**
- **Structure**: Candid look at your work/process
- **Why it works**: Authenticity, human connection
- **Example**: "What my morning routine looks like"

#### 5. **LinkedIn Live**
- **Engagement**: 7x reactions, 24x comments vs regular video
- **Best for**: Q&A, interviews, real-time demonstrations

## Image Design Specifications

### Dimensions Quick Reference

| Image Type | Dimensions | Ratio |
|------------|------------|-------|
| **Feed (mobile-first)** | 1200 x 1350 px | 4:5 |
| **Feed (square)** | 1200 x 1200 px | 1:1 |
| **Feed (landscape)** | 1200 x 627 px | 1.91:1 |
| **Carousel slide** | 1080 x 1080 px | 1:1 |
| **Carousel (vertical)** | 1080 x 1350 px | 4:5 |

**Recommendation**: Use **4:5 vertical** for maximum feed real estate on mobile.

### Design Best Practices

**Colors:**
- Use consistent brand colors
- High contrast for readability
- Avoid overly bright/neon (looks unprofessional)
- Dark backgrounds can stand out in feed

**Text:**
- Minimal text (LinkedIn compresses images)
- Large, readable fonts (min 24pt)
- High contrast against background
- Leave 60-80px padding from edges

**Style:**
- Clean, professional aesthetic
- Not over-designed (feels like an ad)
- Authentic > polished
- Consistent look across posts (brand recognition)

### File Formats
| Format | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| PNG | Graphics, text, screenshots |
| JPG | Photos, complex images |
| PDF | Carousels only |

## Quick Checklist Before Posting

### Images
- [ ] High resolution (min 1080px wide)
- [ ] Correct dimensions for format
- [ ] Text readable on mobile
- [ ] Consistent with brand colors
- [ ] Not cluttered/overwhelming
- [ ] Authentic (not stock-looking)

### Carousels
- [ ] Hook slide is compelling
- [ ] One point per slide
- [ ] Large, readable text
- [ ] Progress indicators
- [ ] CTA on final slide
- [ ] 8-12 slides optimal
- [ ] PDF format uploaded

### Videos
- [ ] Hook in first 3 seconds
- [ ] Captions added
- [ ] Vertical or square format
- [ ] Good audio quality
- [ ] Under 90 seconds (unless high value)
- [ ] Native upload (not link)

## Key Takeaways

1. **Carousels are king** - 24% engagement vs 6.6% for everything else
2. **Vertical (4:5) dominates** - Maximum real estate on mobile
3. **Authenticity beats polish** - Personal photos outperform stock
4. **Video needs captions** - 32% more watch time
5. **First 3 seconds matter** - Hook immediately or lose them
6. **One idea per slide** - Simplicity drives engagement
7. **Data visualizations get shares** - Make complex info digestible
8. **Native formats win** - No external links in posts
